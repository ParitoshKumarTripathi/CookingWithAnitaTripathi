title: Maggi
image: maggi.jpg
desc: Delicious, fast and simple recipe
size: 2-3 servings
time: 1 hour
source: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=T9uI1-6Ac6A
---
1 piece: Maggi
1 pkt: Masala
1/4 tsp: Turmeric
2/3 - 3/4 tsp: Salt

> just boil everything in water for 2 mins with the masala packet given inside the main packet.
###
> then serve it with some sauce, you can also add seasoning of chat masala.
